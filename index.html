My Portfolio

Savita Amaresh Nugadoni is an accomplished Hindustani classical singer, who has been performing regularly since 1987. She has given concerts all over India. She has been invited by the Karnataka Government to perform at such Cultural Festivals as Dasara Utsav, Hampi Utsav, Lakkundi Utsav, Dharwad Utsav etc. She was invited to the National Festival organixed in commemoration of Sri Mallikarjun Mansur in Pune in 2009. She has also been part of Young Musicians Festivals organized in Bangalore, Pune, Nagpur and Tirupati. She showcased her singing talent in Alva's Nudisiri Conference in Mudabidri and Sharana Samskriti Utsav held in Chitradurga. She is a regular invitee to perform at Ganesh Utsavs and Yugadi Utsavs. She was a part of the Sugama Sangeeta Mela Vachana Vaibhava (Light Music Conference) held in Bangalore in 2009. She is a 'B' High Grade artist for All India Radio and performs regularly for Akashavani of Gulbarga, Dharwar etc. She has released several audio recordings of her Hindustani Classical singing and Vachana singing
